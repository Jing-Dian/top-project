Waseda University
In 1913 (Taisho 2), during the 30th anniversary celebration, Chancellor Shigenobu Okuma (at that time) declared the educational principles of Waseda University. The Waseda University Mission Statement, which serves as a fundamental document showing the basic philosophy of education at Waseda University, was drafted by Sanae Takata, Shooyo Tsubouchi, Tameji Amano, Kenkichi Ichijima, Kazutami Ukita, Yasukuni Matsudaira, and others. Shigenobu Okuma reviewed and announced it at the celebration. In 1937 (Showa 12), a monument inscribed with the Mission Statement was erected in front of the main gate of Waseda University, where it stands to this day.
